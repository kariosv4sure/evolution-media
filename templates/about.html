Our Journey

Evolution Media was founded by Emmanuel Okeme, a Computer Science student at Veritas University Abuja. Having mastered the JAMB exam himself, his mission is to teach students the exact system he used to succeed.

What started as a vision to help fellow students has grown into a technology-driven platform focused on digital innovation, marketing, and education. We've helped over 15,000 students achieve exam success and secured 15,000+ admissions into top Nigerian universities like UI, OAU, and UNILAG.

We're not just a tutorial platform — we're your partners in academic success and digital growth.
